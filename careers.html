Careers at ShopEasy

We're a small, fast-moving product team building delightful commerce experiences. While this project is a demo, we welcome collaborators who enjoy shipping clean, useful interfaces.

Open roles (example)

  • Frontend Engineer — React/Vanilla JS experience
  • Product Designer — UI/UX, prototyping, visual design
  • Growth & Content — marketing copy and community

What we value

Curiosity, clean code, strong visual sensibility, and empathy for users. We like people who learn quickly and ship small experiments often.

How to apply

Send a short note, resume, and (optionally) a link to your portfolio or GitHub to jobs@shopeasy.com. We'll review and reply with next steps for promising matches.
